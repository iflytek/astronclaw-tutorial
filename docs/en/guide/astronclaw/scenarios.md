# Application Scenarios

Through built-in skills and automated workflows, AstronClaw can help you quickly complete a variety of complex tasks. Here are a few typical application scenarios:

## 1. Industry Research Report
**Instruction Example:**
> Help me organize the core viewpoints, key data, and future trends of this industry research report, and output 3 investment implications

**Output Effect:**
*   Extract core viewpoints
*   Organize key data
*   Analyze future trends
*   Output investment advice

## 2. News Podcast
**Instruction Example:**
> Help me organize daily hot AI news, generate a 3-minute audio podcast, and push it punctually at 9 AM every day

**Output Effect:**
*   Automatically collect hot news
*   Generate podcast script
*   iFlytek ultra-realistic synthetic voice
*   Scheduled push

## 3. PPT Automatic Generation
**Instruction Example:**
> Make a Chinese PPT on the theme of "New Energy Vehicle Market", accompanied by clear charts and data

**Output Effect:**
*   Market data collection
*   Automatic chart generation
*   Output `.pptx` file
